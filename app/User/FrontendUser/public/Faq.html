FAQ




Where can I find my certificates?



On top of the page, click on the Menu toggler and click on "Certificates"



I have just a few points. How can I earn more points?



You can earn more points by partecipating at the special events. You can find them at the Events' page



How can I partecipate to the events?



You can partecipate in any event just by clicking on "Partecipate" in the Events' page



I've just seen a wounded animal. Is there something I can do?



Yes, you can send an alert through your Personal Page. An operator will reach your position and help the wounded animal
